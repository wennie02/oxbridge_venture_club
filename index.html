Welcome to Oxbridge Venture Club

Connecting global innovation ecosystems through collaboration, inspiration, and shared resources.

About Us

The Oxbridge Venture Club (OVC) is a non-profit organization founded by alumni of Oxford and Cambridge. Established in 2024, the club builds on its roots as a Cambridge student society, continuing to collaborate deeply with the Cambridge Student Union. OVC bridges academia, industry, and society to foster technological innovation and social progress, inspired by the founders' international experiences and their vision to break down informational barriers across regions and industries.

Our Activities

OVC provides a platform for learning, networking, and innovation. Highlights include entrepreneurial hackathons, seminars with industry leaders, and collaborations with prestigious institutions such as Oxford, Cambridge, and LSE. By integrating cross-disciplinary resources and fostering deep industry engagement, OVC drives global innovation and progress.

Our Mission

Inspired by the cultural shifts and structural thinking differences the founders experienced during their international academic journeys, OVC aims to break down cognitive barriers caused by informational gaps. Through collaboration and shared learning, we create opportunities for entrepreneurs and industry leaders to exchange ideas, develop solutions, and advance technological and social progress globally.
